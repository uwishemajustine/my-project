SAMSUNG GALAXY M40

Samsung Galaxy M40 has officially been launched in India after a series of teasers and rumours. The new Galaxy M-series phone is set to go on sale starting June 18 with a competitive price tag of Rs. 19,990. Key highlights of the Galaxy M40 include a full-HD+ Infinity-O Display, triple rear camera setup, and octa-core Snapdragon 675 SoC


LG PHONE X6

LG has now expanded its portfolio by launching the LG X6 smartphone in South Korea. The phone is MIL-STD 810G certified for durablilty. It comes with triple rear cameras aligned horizontally and a waterdrop-notch up front. Key specifications include a MediaTek MT6762 SoC, a 6.26-inch HD+ display, a 13-megapixel selfie sensor, and a 3,500mAh battery. The phone has been launched in two colour options, supports LG Pay, and has in-built Google Assistant support as well. In India, LG is gearing up to launch the W-series and make it available on Amazon India.
